# Brand Intake Question Bank

Use this bank to run a structured interview. Ask one question at a time.

## Fast Intake (8 questions)

1. What is the primary video outcome? (awareness, conversion, activation, retention)
2. Who is the audience and what do they already know?
3. Which platform(s) are we targeting first? (YouTube, Reels, TikTok, ads, product page)
4. Give 3 adjectives for the brand voice and 3 adjectives to avoid.
5. Which existing URL best represents your brand today?
6. Do you have mandatory colors, fonts, and logo usage rules?
7. What should motion feel like? (calm, energetic, premium, playful, technical)
8. What is the hard constraint? (time, approvals, legal, brand strictness)

## Full Intake

## Strategy and outcomes

1. What business KPI should this video influence directly?
2. What decision should the viewer make by the end?
3. What is the single sentence we must make memorable?
4. What is the acceptable tradeoff: fidelity vs speed?

## Audience and distribution

1. Who is the primary audience? (role, expertise, urgency)
2. Where will they watch this first?
3. Sound on or sound off by default?
4. Any localization or accessibility requirements?

## Brand identity

1. What are your canonical brand colors?
2. Are there semantic meanings tied to specific colors?
3. Which typefaces are mandatory, preferred, or forbidden?
4. Is your brand more minimal, expressive, editorial, or utilitarian?
5. Do you prefer dense information or focused single-message scenes?

## Motion language

1. What should the pacing feel like?
2. Do you prefer springy, eased, linear, or mixed motion?
3. Should transitions be subtle cuts, wipes, morphs, or bold scene shifts?
4. Is overshoot acceptable?
5. Should motion prioritize clarity, delight, or drama?

## Narrative and structure

1. Preferred structure: hook -> problem -> proof -> CTA, or another format?
2. What must appear in the first 3 seconds?
3. What should never appear (claims, language, visuals)?
4. What are approved CTA patterns?

## Audio and voice

1. Voiceover style: narrator, founder, expert, energetic sales?
2. How tightly should visuals sync to narration beats?
3. Music constraints (genre, tempo, licensing)?
4. Caption style requirements?

## Governance and operations

1. Who signs off on brand correctness?
2. How many review rounds are realistic?
3. What is the target publish date?
4. What should be parameterized for future videos?

## Technical constraints

1. Primary render formats and dimensions?
2. Max duration per cut?
3. Do we need template-driven variants?
4. Any must-use tooling constraints? (Remotion version, CI, codecs)
