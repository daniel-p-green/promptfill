# Production Rubric (Score Before Final)

Score each category from 0 to 2.

- `0` = fails quality bar
- `1` = acceptable but needs refinement
- `2` = strong

Target score: `>= 16/20` and no category below `1`.

## 1. Narrative Clarity

- 0: Message is unclear or fragmented.
- 1: Message is understandable but not sharp.
- 2: One clear message with logical proof progression.

## 2. Brand Fidelity

- 0: Style conflicts with brand tokens.
- 1: Mostly aligned with occasional drift.
- 2: Strongly aligned across color, typography, and tone.

## 3. Motion Craft

- 0: Motion feels random, noisy, or gimmicky.
- 1: Motion is serviceable but generic.
- 2: Motion supports hierarchy and perceived quality.

## 4. Pacing and Rhythm

- 0: Too rushed or too slow; no cadence control.
- 1: Reasonable pacing with minor rough sections.
- 2: Confident rhythm with intentional pauses and beats.

## 5. Accessibility and Readability

- 0: Contrast/readability failures at common preview sizes.
- 1: Mostly readable with a few risk points.
- 2: Clearly readable, contrast-safe, and reduced-motion aware.

## 6. Platform Safety

- 0: Critical content is cropped in target formats.
- 1: Minor crop risks remain.
- 2: Safe zones respected across all required placements.

## 7. Audio/VO Integration

- 0: Audio competes with visuals or feels disconnected.
- 1: Adequate sync but not intentional.
- 2: Audio beats and visual beats reinforce each other.

## 8. Emotional Arc

- 0: Flat affect; no progression.
- 1: Some arc, limited payoff.
- 2: Clear build and satisfying resolution.

## 9. Technical Stability

- 0: Rendering/performance issues or timing bugs.
- 1: Minor artifacts or edge-case issues.
- 2: Stable renders with clean timing and no visible defects.

## 10. Distinctiveness

- 0: Derivative and forgettable.
- 1: Competent but familiar.
- 2: Distinct within brand constraints and audience expectations.

## Refinement Loop

After scoring:

1. List the three lowest-scoring categories.
2. Apply targeted fixes only for those categories.
3. Re-score and repeat until threshold is met or constraints are exhausted.
